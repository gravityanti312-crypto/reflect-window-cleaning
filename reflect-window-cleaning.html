The Process

Three Steps to Spotless Windows

Our proven 3-step method delivers a streak-free finish every single time — no shortcuts, no mess.

1
🧴

Scrub

We apply our professional-grade cleaning solution with a high-quality wand, loosening every layer of dust, grime, and hard water deposits built up on your glass.

2
🪟

Squeegee

Our technicians use a precision squeegee technique to pull every drop of water and cleaning solution clean off the glass — leaving zero streaks behind.

3
✨

Detail

We hand-dry every edge and corner with a microfiber cloth, clean your tracks and sills, and do a final inspection to make sure your windows are flawless.
