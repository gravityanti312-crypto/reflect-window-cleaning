What We Do

Complete Pool & Spa Care Services

From routine weekly cleanings to green pool rescues and equipment repairs — we handle everything your pool needs to stay safe, beautiful, and swim-ready.

🗓️

Weekly Pool Maintenance

Consistent, scheduled visits every week. We test and balance chemicals, skim debris, brush walls, and vacuum the pool floor so your water stays crystal clear year-round.

🧪

Chemical Balancing & Water Testing

Proper pH, chlorine, alkalinity, and calcium hardness levels keep your pool safe for swimmers and protect your equipment from corrosion and scaling.

🦠

Green Pool Cleanup

Algae overtaken your pool? We specialize in green-to-clean turnarounds — shocking, brushing, and filtering until your water is restored to sparkling blue.

🔧

Equipment Maintenance & Repair

Pump issues, filter problems, or a heater acting up? We diagnose and repair pool equipment to keep everything running efficiently and extend its lifespan.

🧱

Tile Cleaning & Calcium Removal

Hard water deposits and calcium scale build up fast in the Las Vegas Valley. We remove stubborn waterline stains to restore your tile's original shine.

⚗️

Acid Wash Services

When stains go deep into the plaster, an acid wash strips away the top layer to reveal fresh, clean surface. We handle the job safely and thoroughly.

💧

Pool Draining & Refilling

High total dissolved solids (TDS) or excessive stabilizer? A drain-and-refill restores fresh water balance and protects your pool surface and equipment.

🏠

Spa & Hot Tub Cleaning

Spas require specialized care. We clean filters, balance spa chemicals, and inspect jets and heating systems to keep your spa safe and relaxing.

🏚️

Foreclosure & Neglected Pool Cleanup

Inherited a black swamp? We restore completely neglected pools — no matter how bad the condition — back to clean, safe, swimmable water.
