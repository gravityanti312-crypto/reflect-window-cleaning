What We Do

Complete Pool Care Services

From routine weekly cleanings to green pool rescues, LED lighting installs, and equipment repairs — Brian's team handles everything your pool needs to stay safe, beautiful, and swim-ready.

🗓️

Weekly Pool Maintenance

Consistent, scheduled visits every week. We test and balance chemicals, skim debris, brush walls, and vacuum the pool floor so your water stays crystal clear year-round.

🧪

Chemical Balancing & Water Testing

Proper pH, chlorine, alkalinity, and calcium hardness keep your pool safe for swimmers and protect your equipment from corrosion and scaling in Arizona's hard water.

🦠

Green Pool & Algae Cleanup

Algae overtaken your pool? We specialize in green-to-clean turnarounds — shocking, brushing, and filtering until your water is restored to sparkling blue.

🔧

Equipment Repair & Replacement

Pump issues, filter problems, or a heater acting up? We diagnose and repair or replace pool equipment — filters, pumps, heaters, and cleaners — to keep everything running.

🧱

Tile Cleaning & Calcium Removal

Arizona's hard water leaves calcium scale and waterline deposits fast. We remove stubborn buildup to restore your tile's original shine without damaging the surface.

💡

LED Pool Light Installation

Upgrade your pool's ambiance with energy-efficient LED lighting. We handle the full installation so you can enjoy your pool after dark in style.

🔬

Filter Cleaning (DE & Cartridge)

Dirty filters reduce circulation and water clarity. We deep-clean DE and cartridge filters to restore proper flow and keep your system running efficiently.

🧂

Salt Cell Cleaning

Salt systems need periodic cell cleaning to maintain chlorine output. We inspect and clean your salt cell to extend its life and keep your water perfectly balanced.

⚗️

Acid Wash & Pool Draining

When stains go deep or TDS levels get too high, we drain, acid wash, and refill your pool to restore a fresh, clean surface and reset your water chemistry.
